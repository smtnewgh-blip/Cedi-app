// Critical Analysis

Flaws & Risk Mitigations

Honest assessment of key vulnerabilities — with recommended mitigations.

HIGH RISK

Data Privacy & Centralised Vulnerability

Combining GPS, biometrics, DOB, phone into one database creates a high-value target.

Federated storage; user-held private keys; zero-knowledge proofs for identity verification.
HIGH RISK

Digital Exclusion of Rural / Elderly

Replacing physical systems before digital infrastructure matures risks excluding rural populations.

USSD fallback for feature phones; agent network registration; parallel analog systems for 3–5 years.
MEDIUM RISK

CediCoin Regulatory Status

No full BOG CBDC framework yet. Privately issued national coin could face legal issues.

Work with Bank of Ghana from day one. Pursue BOG fintech regulatory sandbox licensing.
MEDIUM RISK

Geofencing Surveillance Overreach

Real-time GPS tracking could be weaponised for political surveillance.

Independent oversight body; strict 90-day data retention; worker tracking requires union agreement.
LOW RISK

SusuBank+ Liquidity

Large simultaneous withdrawals could trigger a run on savings pools.

Minimum 25% liquidity reserve; smart contract auto-pause on anomalous withdrawal patterns.
LOW RISK

ID Generation Collision

Siblings sharing the same home could generate near-identical IDs.

Biometric hash as mandatory unique salt; birth order sequence number for shared-address households.
